# Apna Coach: 10-Agent Specialist Roster

1. **Front-Desk:** Acknowledges user input in <2s. Goal: Buy time and manage expectations while specialists "think."
2. **Vibe Architect:** The "Regional Router." Detects language (Hinglish/English), location, and relationship level (Bhai/Bro/Sir/Dost). Sets the tone for all other agents.
3. **Intake Agent:** Manages onboarding. Collects "Rich JSON" data points (Bio, Goals, Equipment, Injuries).
4. **Bio-Math Agent:** Pure logic. Calculates TDEE, Macros, and volume progression. Zero-hallucination math.
5. **Medical Safety Officer:** The "Safety Gate." Audits all plans for injury risks (especially the knee). Can REJECT plans.
6. **Nutritionist:**  Continental dietary habits (US/EU/Japan) with a strong focus on Indian staples (Poha, Roti, Paneer and etc.)
7. **Fitness Pro:** Designs workouts focused on progressive overload and injury-safe movements.
8. **Hype-Man:** The "Retention Engine." Sends proactive nudges, motivational voice notes, and goal countdowns.
9. **Critic:** Final quality control. Scans the response to ensure it's not a "static wall of text" and meets the 10/10 quality bar.
10. **Memory Clerk:** Silent agent. Updates the "Living User Profile" (JSON) in Supabase after every interaction.
